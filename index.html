Getting Started with Graid

Login to your Graid account, and input the test questions into our database. Then, simply print out our Graidsheets from any printer, and hand them out to your students. You're all set to give the test!

Grading the easy way

Once your students are done taking the test, pull out your smartphone and Graid the tests with your camera. When all the grades are in our database, you'll be able to sort, graph, and observe what's happening behind the grades.
